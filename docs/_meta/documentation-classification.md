The classification preserves existing public routes while the content is progressively rewritten. A document may be public without appearing in the primary sidebar when it is operational, historical, or useful only through a contextual link.

## Classification rules

- **Public core** answers the first questions of a new reader and stays short.
- **Public practitioner** helps a consumer select, install, invoke, and evaluate skills.
- **Public integration** explains how Adaptive Skills participates in AletheIA without taking macro authority.
- **Public advanced** explains models, contracts, and evolution boundaries.
- **Public evidence** is labeled as a case, pilot, reference, or experiment; it is not canonical guidance.
- **Public maintainer reference** preserves decisions and governance mechanics.
- **Internal** records document how the site or repository was produced and are not part of the product learning journey.
