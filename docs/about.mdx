## Design perspective

Neviton works across product design, complex systems, artificial intelligence, and governed agent-assisted work. The projects reflect a design principle: powerful systems become more useful when their boundaries, decisions, evidence, and human responsibilities remain visible.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Column>
    <Panel title="Human-centered">
      Systems should adapt explanation and depth without hiding consequences or removing accountable human decisions.
    </Panel>
  </Column>
  <Column>
    <Panel title="Evidence before claims">
      Guidance, pilots, projections, and delivered capabilities must remain distinguishable and reviewable.
    </Panel>
  </Column>
  <Column>
    <Panel title="Modular by design">
      Governance, reusable capabilities, runtimes, tools, and project-specific policy should interact through explicit boundaries.
    </Panel>
  </Column>
  <Column>
    <Panel title="Continuity over transcript dependence">
      Durable decisions, handoffs, and restartable context should survive beyond one conversation or execution environment.
    </Panel>
  </Column>
</Columns>
