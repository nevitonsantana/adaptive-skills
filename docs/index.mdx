Adaptive Skills is a portable library of small, reusable capabilities for disciplined AI-assisted work.

It helps people and AI agents choose an appropriate way to approach a task, produce a reviewable result, and avoid turning every request into improvised prompting.

## Your first successful use

<Steps>
  <Step title="Understand the boundary">
    Adaptive Skills provides reusable execution guidance. It is not an autonomous agent, runtime, project manager, or approval authority.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install a small set">
    Begin with `workflow`, `feature-planning`, and `testing`, or install only the one skill needed for your task.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose from the task">
    Identify the dominant need: clarify intent, plan, implement, debug, review, validate, communicate, or hand off.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply and verify">
    Use the skill's core moves, produce its expected output, and check its verification criteria before closing the task.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Important boundary

:::note[Skills guide work; they do not authorize it]
A skill can recommend a method, output, or handoff. It does not grant permissions, approve a decision, execute tools by itself, or replace human and project governance.
:::
