# Mid-Round Pause Example

## What Changed
One bounded task slice was completed and validated.

## What Is Now Proved
- the chosen slice worked
- the local proof passed

## What Remains Open
- broader expansion is still not justified
- no cross-boundary work has started yet

## Continue / Stop / Handoff
Stop this round.

## Why This Decision Is Right Now
The objective of the current slice has been met, and continuing would likely turn a bounded round into open-ended scope growth.
